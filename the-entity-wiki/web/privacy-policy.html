Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

The Entity's Wiki ("the App") is a free, fan-made companion application for Dead by Daylight. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, uses, and shares.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

The App runs entirely on your device. It does not require an account, login, or registration. No personal data - including your name, email address, location, or device identifiers - is collected, transmitted, or stored on any external server.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device using your browser's or the app's local storage:

  • Saved builds and loadouts you create
  • Match log entries you record
  • Prestige and progression tracking data
  • App settings and preferences, such as theme and navigation layout

This data never leaves your device. It is not uploaded, synced, or shared with any server or third party. If you uninstall the App, this locally stored data will be deleted.

3. Internet Access

The App does not require an internet connection to function. All game data, images, and content are bundled within the App itself.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash-reporting services. No SDKs that collect user data are included.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, there is no risk of children's data being gathered.

6. Sharing and Disclosure

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data to share, sell, or disclose to any third parties.

7. Build Sharing

The App allows you to generate shareable build codes. These codes are plain text strings that encode loadout information, such as perks, items, add-ons, and offerings. Sharing a build code is entirely at your discretion. The App does not automatically share or transmit these codes anywhere.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
